Essential Extracts
To introduce an AI-powered web browser extension called Linnk that provides summaries, analyses and insights for online articles.
Linnk is a web browser extension that uses AI to analyze articles and provide summaries. It displays a sidebar next to articles with clickable summaries that link to relevant text. The extension extracts key arguments, facts and figures. It collects notes and works across languages. Spoilers are included to preview content. Important numbers, stats and quotes are distilled. Users can gain an overview and navigate directly to points of interest with one click. The goal is to help users dive deep into or skip through content efficiently based on their time and interests.
